About the Show

About Us

Greg

Greg’s love for movies started at a young age watching sci-fi classics and superhero blockbusters. The film which terrified him most was a NOVA special about how the earth was at a 0.0001% chance of being obliterated by gamma rays from a dying star. Now, armed with a better understanding of statistics, Greg enjoys films which question social constructs and unite story, image and soundtrack. In his spare time, he enjoys data science, slow food, tennis and skiing.


Kiki

Kiki enjoys watching film with an analytical eye, with attention to visual details. Armed with a background in visual arts and humanities, she’s interested in understanding how the visuals work in tandem with the narrative to deliver a message about the human condition. In her free time, she likes to cook and bake, go outside, and read.
